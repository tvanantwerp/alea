Alea

Random Password Generator

New passwords will appear here.

Alea is a simple password generator based on selecting several words at random which are easy to memorize but hard to guess.

Alea is inspired by this article by this article by Micah Lee. The word list used by Alea is a random selection of 7,776 words between 5 and 9 characters long taken from this list of 109,582 English words.

This generator is a proof of concept. A skilled adversary could potentially compromise this hosted generator, so you should not totally trust your results. You can download your own copy of Alea and run it from your own PC by visiting the Github repo.
